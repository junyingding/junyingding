Compressor Control and ITCC

Steam turbine (steam turbine) is the power generation and petrochemical

Compressor Control & ITCC

The unit control system is directly related to the performance and reliability of power and petrochemical production. Star Automation provides complete steam turbine control solutions from project consulting, solution planning, engineering implementation, on-site service and long-term project support.

Star Automation has accumulated a wealth of theoretical and practical experience in nearly twenty steam turbine control engineering practices. For example, cogeneration units, especially double-pumping steam turbine units, is the difficulty of unit control, but at the same time, this type of unit is often the petrochemical industry's key power units. Star Automation has successfully combined the world's best control algorithms with the practical application of China's steam turbine users in the key areas of petrochemical, steel and power generation, while absorbing the world's excellent CHP control theory. Successfully implemented a number of key steam turbine control projects at home and abroad, to win the confidence of turbine manufacturers and end users.

Star Automation has been working closely with a number of steam turbine manufacturers including Harbin Turbine Works, Siemens Industrial Turbine, Qingdao Jianteng Steam Turbine Works and many other long-term close relations of cooperation, which makes Star Automation, both in the new Unit or old unit transformation project can combine the characteristics of the unit itself with excellent control algorithm, to provide users with the best solution.

Based on excellent control algorithms, extensive engineering practices and strong long-term technical support, Star Automation has successfully implemented a number of key turbine control projects in power generation, petrochemical, and steel fields, winning turbine manufacturers and ultimately The user's trust.

鞍山钢铁十三号机组
鞍山钢铁十三号机组

鞍山钢铁十三号机组是二十世纪引全套进口汽轮鼓风机组,汽轮机是Sulzer机组，风机是Brown Boveri鼓风机。机组最初的控制系统是机械液压调节方式, 控制精度不好、操作复杂、维护不方便。随着投运时间的增加，机械磨损和控制精度差等问题增多。

控之星自动化采用Woodward公司优秀的505数字控制器和西门子PLC系统,并采用优秀的汽轮机和压缩机防喘算法成功地实施了汽轮机和压缩机综合控制系统改造,不仅使古老的机组能够继续为鞍钢的生产贡献力量,而且提高了机组效率和操作自动化水平。

  • 机组类型:汽轮鼓风机组
  • 机组功率:22.8MW
  • 机组制造商:Sulzer
  • 控制器:505/PLC
  • 项目类型:ITCC
  • 项目日期:2015年
